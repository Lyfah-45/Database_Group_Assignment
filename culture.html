What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These six principles underpin every decision, interaction, and initiative at Nexus Corporation.

01

Integrity

We act with honesty and transparency in everything we do. We hold ourselves accountable and do the right thing — even when no one is watching.

02

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in our work. Continuous improvement is not optional — it is how we honour the trust placed in us.

03

Inclusion

We celebrate diversity of thought, background, and identity. Every voice matters, and every employee deserves to feel a sense of belonging.

04

Collaboration

We achieve more together. By sharing knowledge and supporting one another, we unlock potential that no individual could reach alone.

05

Innovation

We embrace change and challenge convention. Our best ideas come from curiosity, courage, and a willingness to try new approaches.

06

Accountability

We take ownership of our actions and outcomes. When we commit, we deliver — and when we fall short, we learn and improve.

Workplace Standards

Code of Conduct & Policies

Nexus Corporation is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and professional workplace for all employees. The following policies apply to every member of our organisation, at all levels.

Anti-Harassment & Discrimination Policy

We are committed to providing a workplace free from harassment, bullying, and discrimination. This includes protection on the basis of race, gender, age, religion, disability, sexual orientation, or any other characteristic.

  • All reports are treated with strict confidentiality
  • A dedicated HR liaison is available for all complaints
  • Zero-tolerance approach to retaliation against reporters
  • Investigations are conducted fairly and within 10 business days

Disciplinary Framework

The following progressive disciplinary steps are followed in cases of misconduct:

  1. Verbal Warning — Documented discussion with line manager regarding the issue.
  2. Written Warning — Formal written notice issued and placed on personnel file.
  3. Final Written Warning — Last formal notice before escalation; review period activated.
  4. Suspension & Investigation — Temporary suspension pending a formal disciplinary hearing.
  5. Dismissal — Termination of employment following due process and hearing outcome.

Important Note

Serious misconduct (gross insubordination, fraud, violence) may result in immediate suspension and hearing without prior warnings, as per the Labour Code of Lesotho.
