Our Commitment

Learning & Development Policy

Nexus Corporation is committed to the continuous professional development of all employees. We allocate a minimum of 40 training hours per employee per year and invest M4.2 million annually in learning initiatives.

Our Learning & Development (L&D) strategy is aligned with individual performance goals, departmental objectives, and the broader corporate strategy. Every employee has a right to a Personal Development Plan (PDP), reviewed bi-annually with their line manager.

Policy Principles

  • All permanent employees are eligible for structured L&D support
  • Development plans are co-created by the employee and their manager
  • Training investments are tied to business-relevant outcomes
  • External study assistance is available for accredited programmes
  • Internal knowledge-sharing is encouraged and rewarded

Your Growth Plan

Personal Development Planning

A Personal Development Plan (PDP) is a structured framework for employees to identify their professional goals, growth areas, and action plans — in alignment with the organisation's objectives.

The PDP Process

  1. Self-Assessment — Reflect on your current skills, accomplishments, and development areas.
  2. Goal Setting — Define 2–4 SMART development goals for the year with your line manager.
  3. Action Planning — Identify specific activities, training, and milestones to achieve each goal.
  4. Mid-Year Review — Track progress against your plan at the 6-month mark.
  5. Year-End Evaluation — Assess overall achievement and set goals for the following year.

PDP Form & Support

Download the PDP Template (HR-LD-01) from the HR Portal or request it from your HR Business Partner. Completed PDPs must be submitted within 30 days of the annual review cycle commencement.
