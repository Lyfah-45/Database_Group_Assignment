How We Hire

Our Recruitment Process

We are committed to a fair, transparent, and efficient recruitment process for all candidates — internal and external alike.

01

Application Submission

Submit your CV and cover letter via the HR Portal or email recruitment@nexuscorp.co.ls by the stated deadline.

02

Shortlisting & Screening

Our recruitment team reviews all applications within 5 business days. Shortlisted candidates are contacted for a 20-minute phone screen.

03

Panel Interview

Successful candidates are invited to a structured panel interview with the hiring manager and an HR representative.

04

Assessment / Technical Task

Depending on the role, candidates may complete a written assessment, technical exercise, or case study presentation.

05

Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates receive a formal offer letter. Background and reference checks are completed prior to a confirmed start date.

Application Requirements

  • Updated CV (PDF format, max 4MB)
  • Tailored cover letter addressing the role
  • Certified copies of academic qualifications
  • National ID or passport copy
  • Three professional references with contact details

Internal Applicants

Internal applications must be accompanied by a letter of support from your line manager.
