Employee Benefits

A comprehensive overview of the benefits, leave entitlements, and wellness support available to all Nexus employees.

What We Offer

Your Total Rewards Package

At Nexus Corporation, we invest in the wellbeing of every employee — financially, physically, and emotionally.

❤️

Medical Aid

Comprehensive medical cover for employees and up to 4 dependants. Nexus contributes 60% of the monthly premium. Administered by PULA Medical Aid Fund.

📈

Pension & Provident Fund

Employee contributes 7.5% of gross salary; Nexus matches with 10%. Vesting period of 3 years. Administered by Old Mutual Lesotho.

🏠

Housing Allowance

Qualifying employees at Grade 6 and above receive a monthly housing allowance of 15% of basic salary, subject to HR approval.

🚘

Transport Allowance

A monthly transport allowance of M1,200 is provided to all permanent employees. Field staff are entitled to vehicle use or fuel reimbursement.

🎓

Study Assistance

Nexus sponsors up to 80% of tuition fees for approved job-relevant qualifications. Applications must be submitted to HR at least 60 days prior to enrolment.

💕

Employee Wellness Programme

Free access to professional counselling, mental health support, and a 24-hour employee assistance helpline through our EAP provider.

🎻

Group Life Insurance

All permanent employees are automatically enrolled in group life cover of 3x annual salary at no cost to the employee. Beneficiary nomination forms available via HR.

📱

Communication Allowance

Staff in client-facing and field roles receive a monthly communication allowance of M800 to cover work-related phone usage.

🍽

Subsidised Canteen

Employees enjoy 50% subsidised meals at the on-site canteen Monday through Friday. Nutritious options prepared fresh daily by our catering team.

Leave Request Procedure

All leave requests must be submitted via the HR Portal or on the official Leave Request Form (HR-LV-01), signed by the employee and approved by their line manager before leave commences. Retrospective leave applications may not be approved.

Work Arrangements

Remote Work Policy

Effective 1 April 2025, Nexus Corporation has adopted a hybrid work model for eligible employees. This policy governs remote work arrangements, expectations, and entitlements.

Eligibility

  • Permanent employees who have completed their probationary period (minimum 6 months)
  • Roles designated as hybrid-eligible by the line manager and HR
  • Employees with a satisfactory performance record (no active written warnings)
  • Employees who have completed the Remote Work Readiness Assessment

Hybrid Schedule

Eligible employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week, with a mandatory minimum of 3 days in the office. The specific days must be agreed with the line manager and submitted to HR monthly.

Remote Work Expectations

  1. Availability — Must be contactable during core hours (09:00–16:00) via approved communication channels.
  2. Security — All work must be conducted on a secure, password-protected network. Company VPN must be active at all times.
  3. Equipment — Company-issued equipment must be used. Personal devices may only be used with prior IT approval.
  4. Productivity — Output expectations remain unchanged. Managers may request output reports for remote days.
  5. Data Protection — No confidential company data may be stored on personal cloud services or devices.

Remote Work Agreement

All remote work arrangements must be formalised via the Remote Work Agreement Form (HR-RW-02), signed by the employee, line manager, and HR. Contact hr@nexuscorp.co.ls to obtain the form.
